---
title: Text to Speech
description: Text-to-speech models for generating audio to drive VRM lip-sync.
---

You can use text-to-speech models to generate audio and feed it into `useVRMVowelAnalyser` for lip-sync.

- <a href="https://huggingface.co/models?pipeline_tag=text-to-speech" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Hugging Face TTS Models</a> — Browse text-to-speech models on Hugging Face.
- <a href="https://huggingface.co/spaces/John6666/mikuTTS" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">mikuTTS</a> — Try text-to-speech directly in the browser.
